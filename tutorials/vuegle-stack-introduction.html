vuegle stack introduction

So what vuegle stack is about?

The formula is simple, vuegle = vuejs + google apps script. The vuejs framework is used for the front-end part and the google apps script is used to handle the backend part.


Why vue js ?

Actually why not, maybe because it is the most simple and progressive javascript framework that can be installed and deployed in a seconds. Or maybe because it's the most beloved framework by most of the developers. Have i mentioned vue have amazing community support and alot of cool utility libraries.


What about google apps script?

Google Apps Script is just a platform that let you to automate google products using javascript. You can also deploy your own apis. And what is cool about it is that it is for free.
